Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice and meat, such as chicken, goat, lamb, etc. It is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, and is originated from North India. Biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat (Chicken, goat, lamb)
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
